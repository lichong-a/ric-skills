# Mobile Generation And Review

Use this reference for the app design bible, prompt plan, regeneration, and independent review package.

## App Design Bible

Keep it compact:

- Product and direction statement.
- Platform strategy.
- Type, color, spacing, radius, and surface roles.
- Navigation model.
- Key components and state language.
- Image, icon, and motion-implied language.

## Screen Manifest

For each screen, record:

- Flow and state.
- Platform and viewport.
- Required controls and content.
- Relationship to previous and next states.
- Generated asset or screen file.
- Known ambiguity.

## Prompt Focus

Describe the user task, screen state, hierarchy, platform strategy, and selected design bible. Exclude watermarks, fake brands, unreadable critical text, distorted device regions, and incoherent controls.

## Quality Review

Inspect at realistic device scale:

- Readability and touch-target plausibility.
- Safe areas and system regions.
- Navigation and flow continuity.
- Consistent components and state language.
- Product specificity and visual hierarchy.
- No accidental text, watermark, fake brand, or generic filler.

## Regeneration

Regenerate the smallest failed unit. State the defect and preserve the approved design bible. Do not recreate a complete flow because one screen failed.

## Evidence Package

Provide:

- Brief and platform decision.
- App design bible.
- Flow board or contact sheet.
- Screen manifest and provenance.
- Selected and rejected direction rationale.
- Full-resolution selected screens.
- Known limitations.

Send this package to `ric-visual-design-review`; send implementation-bound screen/state pairs to `ric-design-qa`.
