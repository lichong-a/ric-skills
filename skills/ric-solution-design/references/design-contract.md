# Design Contract

## Required Design Evidence

- Approved requirements and acceptance artifact versions.
- Existing-system constraints and preserved behavior.
- Selected approach, alternatives, and tradeoffs.
- Component boundaries, interfaces, data flow, state transitions, and permission model.
- Failure modes, recovery, concurrency, security, observability, and operations.
- Migration, compatibility, rollout, and rollback strategy when relevant.
- Test strategy and acceptance-evidence plan.
- Work packages with scope, dependencies, integration order, and expected outputs.

## Visual Direction Rule

For public, immersive, branded, redesign, ImageGen-led, or high-visual surfaces, create three directions that differ in hierarchy, composition, product framing, asset strategy, and motion language. Palette-only variants do not count.

Design review fails when the design cannot be implemented without major unstated decisions or when acceptance criteria cannot be traced to the design and test strategy.
