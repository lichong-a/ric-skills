# Visual Review Rubric

Score each category from `1` to `5`, then calculate the weighted total:

- Product and audience specificity: `20`.
- Task hierarchy and action clarity: `20`.
- Distinctiveness and art direction: `20`.
- System consistency and craft: `15`.
- Responsive, state, and accessibility quality: `15`.
- Asset quality and source fidelity: `10`.

## Review Evidence

Inspect relevant desktop and narrow viewports, loading/empty/error states, permissions, long content, overlays, generated assets, themes, and locales.

Treat genericity, imbalance, weak hierarchy, template-like repetition, inappropriate motion, poor asset quality, and product mismatch as findings. Known anti-patterns are evidence, not automatic failures.

Report the score, category rationale, blocking findings, focused crops or screenshot references, and the recommended next gate.
