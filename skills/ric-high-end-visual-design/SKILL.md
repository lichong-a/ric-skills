# RIC High-End Visual Design

## Role

This is a visual **modifier**. It supplies direction and critique criteria but never owns routing, chooses randomly, or overrides the primary executor and approved requirements.

## Principles

- Build premium quality from hierarchy, typography, spacing, material, imagery, state craft, and restraint.
- Explore three genuinely different directions for high-visual work when required by the design gate.
- Use visual assets and motion only when they reinforce product meaning or brand recognition.
- Respect the existing design system, accessibility, content density, performance budget, and target audience.
- Treat utility surfaces differently from immersive, portal, login, or branded workbench surfaces.

## Quality Gate

The independent visual reviewer selects or rejects directions. High-visual surfaces require at least `82/100`; utility surfaces require at least `75/100`. A modifier cannot approve itself.
