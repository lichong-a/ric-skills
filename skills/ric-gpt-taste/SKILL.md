# RIC GPT Taste

## Role

Use as a visual **modifier**. Direction selection must be brief-driven and independently reviewed; never simulate tool execution, randomly select a direction, redirect ownership, or claim generated assets or browser validation that did not run.

## Direction

- Explore materially different composition, typography, imagery, and motion options when the design gate requires alternatives.
- Favor a distinct focal moment, coherent brand expression, and deliberate pacing.
- Use GSAP, Motion, WebGL, or 3D only when they improve meaning, navigation, state understanding, or brand recognition.
- Provide reduced-motion behavior and performance evidence for advanced motion.

## Boundaries

Utility admin pages remain dense and efficient. Avoid placeholder galleries, generic slogans, decorative perpetual loops, and motion applied to every element.

## Verification

Use `ric-visual-design-review`, `ric-design-qa`, and `ric-acceptance-validation` through `ric-delivery-loop`.
